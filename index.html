Mission

Udhaviduvom Manitha Arakkatalai is dedicated to building a brighter tomorrow, one helping hand at a time. Our mission is to provide vital support and assistance tothose facing adversity, guided by a commitment to compassion, integrity, and community empowerment. Together with our dedicated volunteers, we strive to uplift lives and foster a more compassionate society through our unwavering dedication to serving others.
